# Customer support app

A customer support Agent App can declare:

- Product and policy Knowledge templates.
- Entries for answer drafting, escalation, and policy lookup.
- Tool requirements for CRM or ticket systems.
- Artifact contracts for reply drafts and escalation notes.
- Evals for policy compliance, source grounding, and tone.

This demonstrates that Agent App is not limited to content production.
